# Roadmap

## Planned Future Features

1. **User accounts**
   - Parent sign-in and optional child profile switching.
2. **Parent PIN**
   - Protect parent dashboard with a simple PIN lock.
3. **Uploading audio stories**
   - Secure upload + metadata form with format validation.
4. **AI-generated story scripts**
   - Parent-assisted generation drafts aligned to biblical references and age level.
5. **Bible verse memory mode**
   - Repeat-after-me, phrase highlighting, and progress stickers.
6. **Offline mode**
   - Download approved stories for travel and low-connectivity use.
7. **Physical NFC/card integration idea**
   - Optional mapped NFC cards that launch specific stories in app.
8. **Subscription model**
   - Free starter pack + premium curriculum bundles.
9. **Church/school version**
   - Classroom playlists, lesson packs, and teacher controls.
10. **Multi-child profiles**
    - Per-child favorites, age filters, and listening history.
11. **Content moderation + theological review workflow**
    - Editorial queue, doctrine review checklist, and versioned approvals.
